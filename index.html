About the Game

rush.io is a brutally fast FPS where movement is everything. Inspired by Krunker and old-school shooters, it’s all about bhopping, precision, and reflex. No mercy. Just movement and mastery.

Key Features

  • Fluid bhop and movement system
  • Multiple competitive maps
  • Responsive controls and tight gunplay
  • Real-time kill log & leaderboard (coming soon)
  • Skins, settings, and private lobbies (WIP)
